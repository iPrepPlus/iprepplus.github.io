About iPrepPlus

Oct 17th, 2015 2:04 pm

  • iPrep Plus is a company of educators who know HOW to help students get the results they are striving for
  • Practicing and retired teachers, principals, associate superintendents, curriculum specialists, and provincial Teacher in Excellence Award winners are using their expertise and experience to develop quality, effective, electronic review packages for students that provide home support to in-class learning

WHAT IS iPREP PLUS?

  • iPrep Plus is a unique home support to classroom learning for English 30-1, Social 30 -1, Biology 30, Chemistry 30, Math 30-1, Math 30-2, Physics 30, and Science 30
  • All electronic review resources of 50-150 pages and support videos, each 5-10 minutes in length, are developed by well-respected teachers with a reputation for excellence in teaching and achieving exceptional results with students in the classroom and on diploma examinations

WHY SHOULD I BE INTERESTED IN iPREP PLUS?

  • iPrep Plus provides you with a complete, structured, and comprehensive review of course material and short video clips to help you understand some of the most challenging concepts of a course
  • For approximately the same cost as one hour of face-to-face tutoring, you can have unlimited access to the resources for one iPrep Plus subject
  • You and your school are responsible for 70% of your final grade – maximize your potential! Use the materials at home to support your course review, class assignments, and exam preparation.
  • Take advantage of the iPrep Plus limited time, introductory price offer

WHEN/WHERE CAN I GET THE iPREP PLUS SUPPORT RESOURCES?

  • iPrep Plus electronic resource packages can be accessed anytime, anywhere and on any device!
  • Log onto the iPrep Plus web site www.iPrepPlus.com for information and introductory pricing
  • Start using the resource review package immediately to help with classroom course assignments, unit tests, and quizzes. Use the materials multiple times for the cost of one hour of traditional tutoring!
