DIPLOMAX Co-founder Launches New Service – iPREP PLUS

Oct 20th, 2015 7:21 pm

DIPLOMAX co-founder Noel Kunst is pleased to announce the launch of iPrep Plus – Home Support to Classroom Learning. It is a province-wide, complementary service to the popular face-to-face review sessions of DIPLOMAX which are held twice a year in Edmonton to assist students in preparing for the provincial diploma exams.

iPrep Plus provides students with unlimited access to an electronic Study Notes package and Video Support for the “tough topics” in English 30-1, social Studies 30-1, Biology 30, Chemistry 30, Math 30-1, Math 30-2, Physics 30, and Science 30. The resources can be used anytime, on any device, anywhere to support students in their day-to-day studies, class assignments, quizzes and unit or final tests. Now that 70% of final grades are assigned by the school, it is more important than ever that students have access to quality materials to support their home study.

The electronic Study Notes of 50-150 pages provides a focused and comprehensive review of all course content. The Video Support, comprised of five to ten minutes videos, deals with the “tough topics” of the course identifying common problems or errors students make, and providing a summary of helpful suggestions and hints to overcome and master the challenges. The best news about iPrep Plus is that students can have immediate and unlimited access to all this for the same cost as one hour of traditional tutoring!!

Check out the web site www.iPrepPlus.com for the introductory price offer and many features!
