Study Skills Personal Inventory

Mar 14th, 2016 5:23 pm

There is a difference between homework and studying. Homework is assigned by the teacher and has specific instructions and a “due date”. Studying is initiated by you – you determine what to study, when and where to do your studying and how long you are going to study the material covered in class. You can maximize your potential by having a study schedule and knowing where to focus your efforts.

The purpose of this personal inventory is to help you become familiar with your study habits and attitudes. It is intended to help you identify areas where you may wish to focus on improving your skills. Read each statement and consider how it applies to you. Keep track of your responses for each statement.
